Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards and debit cards through our secure payment processor. Your billing information is encrypted and protected.

Can I change my plan anytime?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately and billing adjusts accordingly.

What happens if I cancel?

You'll continue to have access to your paid features until the end of your current billing period. Your data is safely stored and you can reactivate anytime.

Do you offer customer support?

Yes! All plans include customer support. Pro subscribers get priority support with faster response times and dedicated assistance.
